What is Your History?

Building out your character's personal history is as simple as answering as rolling D666 on the Personal History Table. First roll on the Vocation column and add the Vocation Talent to your character sheet along with the Vocation you rolled. Then roll once for each of the other columns and note on the results on your character sheet, filling out the details.
