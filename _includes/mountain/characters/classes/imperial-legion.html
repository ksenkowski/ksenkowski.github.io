Imperial Guardsman

Drawn primarily from men of the frozen north, Imperial Guardsman are frightening in both appearance and equipment. They attack their enemies with reckless rage, neither caring about the loss of blood or their wounds. Coupled with their unwavering loyalty to the Emnperadors, they are regarded as the most fearsome enemies any man could come to know.
