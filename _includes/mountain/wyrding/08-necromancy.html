Animation Ritual

The Animation Ritual allows you to command a corpse to rise up and follow orders. The base Difficulty of this Ritual is determined by the type plus the level of decomposition and the type of undead.

Success creates an animated minion that lasts until it is dismissed or destroyed. When used in a Skirmish it takes a number of Rounds equal to the Difficulty. Failure always results in a Twist or Condition dtermined by the GM.
