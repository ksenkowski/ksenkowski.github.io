Dominance Ritual

The Dominance Ritual allows a character to take control of a Taint Sinkhole or an individual. The Difficulty of this Ritual's Wyrding Challenge is equal to the Sinkhole's Strength or the target's Temperament roll.

Success puts the Power of the Sinkhole at the character's disposal or forces the individual to follow your every command until a Purification Ritual is successfully used on the individual or they are killed. When used in a Skirmish it takes a number of Rounds equal to the Difficulty. Failure always results in a Twist or Condition dtermined by the GM.
