Adversaries

The road to the Mountain is populated with all manner of monsters and people who would stand in the Cell's way. Adversaries are broken into three categories; the Fey, the Tainted, and the Mundane. Within those categories is a wide variety of opponents and allies the Cell could encounter on their journey.
