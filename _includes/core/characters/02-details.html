Character Details

Fleshing out your character's personal details simply requires you pull 4 random Runes out, toss them and note which are face up and face down. You then assign each of these Runes to one of the following tables and note the results.

The Dirty Secrets table works to to provide an air of mystery surrounding your character. The heros of Conspiracy of Shadows are flawed individuals and these secrets help bring them to life. If the Dirty Secret involves a person, add that person to your Network.
