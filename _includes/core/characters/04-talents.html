Select Talents

When creating a character you create two talents that help to define your character. Talents are a brief description of something the character is capable and even good at doing. It is important that they describe only one capability.

Each Talent is also associated with a Rune. When writing down the Talent also include this Rune. If while using this Talent during a toss that Rune appears face-up, the character gets a +1s.

Example Talents

  • Confuse the minds of others with words and gestures
  • Trained to utilize agility and flexibility to move quietly
  • Jumps and maintains balance like a cat
  • Can tap into the Wyrding to shape the world
  • Purified and sanctified against Taint
  • Has a seemingly endless reservoir of physical endurance
  • Can kill the feeble minded with a word
  • Can transfer Wyrds onto scrolls for others to use
  • Has studied the ways of the Alfar and knows their weaknesses
