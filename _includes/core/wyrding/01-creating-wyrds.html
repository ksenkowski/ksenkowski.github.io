The Wyrd

The Wyrd is the way in which individuals tap into supernatural powers to change the world around them. It takes on two forms, the Incantations and Rituals. If a character has a supernatural Talent (e.g. Alchemist, Cultist, Wise Woman, Herbalis, Necromancer, Wyrind) they can perform Incantations. Rituals are esoteric processes that anyone can participate in.

Wyrds of all types have five basic components; a subject, an effect, a duration, the cost and the runes.
