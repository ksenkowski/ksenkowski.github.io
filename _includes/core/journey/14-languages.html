Languages

The Mountain is a game about a journey through exotic lands with a wide variety of cultures. While the Cell comes from a core of six cultures that found in the Great City, the peoples and cultures they will come across will vary. Keeping that in mind, the following conditions are always in play:

  • Characters that speak the same language suffer no penalties communicating.
  • Characters that speak a language from the same language group, but not the same language, suffer a -1R in Challenges and Skirmishes when communication is relevant.
  • Characters that have no common language must resort to extreme measures to communicate and suffer a -4R in Challenges and Skirmishes when communication is relevant.
  • Characters cannot learn the roots of all languages in the world (Djinni, Alfar, Skalfar and Jutunar) without a specific talent like Polygot.

Langauges are grouped under the root tongues, then again within their regional divergent groups. The following illustrates these groups.

Alfar

  • Alfen
    Orun, Irun, Barun, Davrun, Luvilun
  • Lyric
    Hellas, Zeher
  • Imperial
    Avagoni, Castanata, Falkan, Nis, Vlesi, Vorish

Djinni

  • Duni
    Ayun, Hazan, Hanud, Ranka
  • Hikemi
    Umarsi, Vessasi, Larjeti, Zimbish, Bemi

Jutun

  • Jutuni
    Eskari, Isoli, Navari, Omeroni, Tarathan
  • Valadarin
    Bravish, Daskan, Sarl, Toloskan, Vashish

Skalfar

  • Skaren
    Anab, Inga, Kol, Salot, Skari, Uar
  • Horek
    Besh, Kida, Nardosh, Otash
