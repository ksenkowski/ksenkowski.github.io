all sin has it's seeds sown in ambition

Twelve years ago I published my first table top role-playing game, Conspiracy of Shadows. I had been working on it for years prior and it has morphed and changed over time with a second release, a companion book, and then in 2012 I ran a Kickstarter for Conspiracy of Shadows: Apprentice.

Since that time I have been tinkering with a new version of the game, taking what I learned from the writing of a non-standard role-playing game and mixing it with my love for traditional games. In 2016 I will be creating hand-made boxed sets for the game as a part of my Patreon, but in the mean time you can find a preview of the rules here.
