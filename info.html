making magic happen

Hi, my name is Keith Senkowski and I am an illustrator, author, technologist and game designer. Conspiracy of Shadows is the playground where I share my work and thoughts on things that are important to me.

I spend most of my days thinking and working in the mobile-first responsive web design space. Being a technologist with a design background has made that front of mind and I am excited to see more and more designers in the digital space embracing the fluidity of the medium.
