Ilvabir Heldevadus

Ilvabir was the Kur of the Ostarathi Kurdom of Greater Voria from 223 to 224 2E. A member of the powerful, but non-royal Tarathi clan of Heldevadi, he took advantage of a relationship with the invading Imperial legato Bermudo to facilitate his election to the throne.
