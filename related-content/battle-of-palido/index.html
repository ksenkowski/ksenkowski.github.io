The Hatarathi

The Hatarathi were a Tarathi people from the high plains in the *Unknowable East*. They are first mentioned in the works of Zellas as confederates of the Karatarathi, who at the time of his writing [ The Epistles to Zeno, 1344 2E ], they had taken the Hegoi Wall had overrun Ilaria [ Later Zerem ], Sloria [ Later Karatan ], and were threatening Greater Voria.
